Eiyuu no Uta (2014)
Eiyuu no Uta (英雄の詩, Song of The Hero) is the opening theme for Ultraman Ginga S. The song also served as the fourth opening theme for New Ultraman Retsuden both during and after Ginga S aired.

Artist: THE ALFEE
Released: 2014
Album: Ultraman Ginga S original Soundtrack
